How payment works

We keep this simple and handle it directly with you — no card form on this site.

We arrange payment over email

We don't take card details or payments through the website. Once we've scoped your project together, we'll follow up by email to sort out the details:

  1. We agree on the package and price (see pricing).
  2. We'll email you an invoice and walk through the payment method that works best for you.
  3. Work starts once that's confirmed — you'll always know the price before anything is charged.

If you've already started a project with us and have a payment question, just reply to any email thread we've had, or reach out below.

Questions about payment? Email us at hello@launchstudio.co
